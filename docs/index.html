Features

Kindle Integration

Extracts highlights and notes directly from your Kindle notebook page.

Notion Export

Exports highlights as Notion quote blocks, with color mapping and summary counts.

Secure & Private

Your Notion token is stored locally and securely. No tracking, no ads.

Easy to Use

Simple pop-up UI for configuration and export. No setup headaches.
