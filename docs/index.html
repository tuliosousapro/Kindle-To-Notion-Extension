Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is it really free?

Yes! Kindle to Notion is 100% free and open source. No premium tiers, no hidden costs, no subscriptions.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Your data never leaves your device. We don't have servers, don't collect data, and can't see your highlights. It's just you and Notion.

Do I need a Notion account?

Yes, you need a free Notion account to use this extension. Notion is where your highlights will be saved.

Does it work with my Amazon region?

We support 12 regions: US, UK, Canada, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Japan, Australia, India, Mexico, and Brazil.

What if I already exported a book?

No problem! Re-export anytime. The extension detects existing highlights and only adds new ones - no duplicates.

Need help setting up?

Check our documentation for step-by-step guides, or open an issue on GitHub.
